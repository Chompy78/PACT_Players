---
title: No One Stays a Child
description: Campaign pitch and premise
---

*A campaign for levels 0 to 14.*

You're eight years old, and the sheep are missing from the woodline again.

This campaign opens with four sessions of childhood in Amble, a small farming village where everyone knows everyone. You'll play the same character from age eight to sixteen — building a den with the other village kids, getting into scrapes you didn't start, and slowly working out who your real friends are. You'll grow up alongside Wren, reckless and full of secrets, and Bram, always trying a little too hard to matter.

Character creation happens gradually, in the fiction rather than all at once — you start as a child with a few simple traits, and your full character comes together as you play, the way a person actually grows into who they are.

This is just the opening chapter. Amble won't stay simple forever — the wider world is already watching, and debts get made before anyone understands what they'll cost. The choices your character makes as a child are the ones the story holds them to later: who they become here will keep shaping their path for years, maybe decades, to come. For now, though, expect warmth, comedy, and a village worth loving before any of that comes due.

Bring a character concept, not a character sheet.
