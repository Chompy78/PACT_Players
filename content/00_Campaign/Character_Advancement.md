---
title: A Note on Character Advancement
description: How character growth works differently in this campaign
---

PACT uses the familiar D&D rules for rolling dice, making checks, casting spells, and fighting monsters. What changes is how your character grows. Instead of gaining a fixed package of abilities each time you level up, you earn Advancement Points (AP) as the story unfolds and spend them on the features, skills, spells, and abilities that matter to your character.

Your character isn't locked into a class progression. As you advance, you choose which abilities to learn and which paths to pursue, building a character that reflects their experiences and priorities. Whether you focus on a single archetype or combine abilities from several, every character develops differently because every improvement is chosen, not automatically granted.
